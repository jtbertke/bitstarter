FAQ

What is "Game of Thrones"?
"Game of Thrones" is an epic fantasy television drama. Based on the "A Song of Ice and Fire" book series by George R.R. Martin, and described as 'The Sopranos set in Middle-Earth', "Game of Thrones" follows the storyline of many characters as thy vie for authority of the Iron Throne - the seat of power for the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. Filled with betrayal, sex, revenge, and just a touch of magic; "Game of Thrones" is one of the most watched programs on television today, and for a good reason! Visit HBO.com or wikipedia.org for more information, but beware of spoilers!
What are the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros?
Namely - The North, The Riverlands, The Vale, The Westerlands, The Stormlands, The Reach, and Dorne. Also set apart in Westeros are the Iron Islands, and the Crownlands (the immediate area surrounding the capital, King's Landing). Each of the kingdoms is ruled by a great house - House Stark for The North, House Tully for The Riverlands, House Arryn for The Vale, House Lannister for The Westerlands, House Baratheon for The Stormlands, House Tyrell for The Reach, and House Martell for Dorne. The lord of House Stark serves as Warden of the North, of House Lannister as Warden of the West, of House Arryn as Warden of the East, and of House Tyrell as Warden of the South. There is also one "King of Westeros", styled "King of the Andals and the First Men, Lord of the Seven Kingdoms and Protector of the Realm".
Why are we predicting who will die next? -or- Why do so many characters die?
So many characters die because George R.R. Martin is a sick, cruel bastard with no heart whatsoever. Only joking of course. Mr. Martin kills off so many of his characters because - and these are his words, not mine - he wants us to be afraid to turn the page. However, he also knows that we will. Killing characters is an incredibly effective strategy to sell books because everyone wants to know if the characters they love will prevail, and if those that they hate will die. Another effective way to sell books is by writing the books with beautiful description, which he does. But, just as a reminder, this site is meant to be used by non-book readers who do not know the future events of the show.
